## pi-herdr-subagents

The asynchronous Herdr jobs extension borrows orchestration ideas and notification/widget formatting from [`0xRichardH/pi-herdr-subagents`](https://github.com/0xRichardH/pi-herdr-subagents/tree/d654eae75ff347ccb618113f2af85f3040d9ade9), including its fire-and-forget watcher and steer-notification pattern. `pi-tools` implements an independent, command-focused runner; it is not a fork of that project.

Source examined: [`d654eae75ff347ccb618113f2af85f3040d9ade9`](https://github.com/0xRichardH/pi-herdr-subagents/tree/d654eae75ff347ccb618113f2af85f3040d9ade9).
